# What this is

A note-taking app where the layout is alive.

No pages. No navigation. No sidebar. Just a spatial field of weighted nodes that expand and compress as you interact.

Click to focus. Click elsewhere to return. Search to deform the space toward what you need. Edit inline — if a node has enough weight (space), it's editable.

Everything is a folder or a note. Nest them freely. The structure IS the interface.
